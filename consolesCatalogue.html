Consoles Catalogue

Video Game Consoles

Video games have been around almost as long as computers themselves. Since their inception, a plethora of innovations and new technologies have advanced the complexity and interactivity of video games to the point they are becoming indistinguishable from reality. Below is a catalogue of the latest consoles available today to get started playing and enjoying video games now.

The Playstation 5 is the latest console released by Sony on November 12, 2020.
Playstation 5 Features:

  • A super-fast SSD for immediate game loading
  • Hardware accelerated ray-tracing
  • Backwards compatibility with most Playstation 4 titles
There are 2 varients of the Playstation 5:
  • Playstation 5 base model
  • Playstation 5 Digital Edition
The Playstation 5 has enjoyed immense success since launching a few years ago. Major titles showing the full power and capabilities of the hardware have since been released.
Major Playstation 5 Games:
  • Ratchet and Clank: Rift Apart
  • Returnal
  • God of War: Ragnarok
  • Demon's Souls (2020)
The Playstation 5 is a feature rich console with a wide variety of game genres for gamers of all types to chose from. The power of the custom processors inside the Playstation 5 provides awe inspiring visuals and gameplay features never before possible.

The Xbox Series X|S are the latest consoles released by Microsoft on November 10, 2020. There are two distinct Xbox consoles to chose from this generation. The flagship product is the Xbox Series X.
Xbox Series X features:

  • A powerful processor for playing games at high resolutions and framerates
  • A blue-ray disk drive
  • An internal SSD with 1TB capacity
The Xbox Series S is a budget oriented option to get gamers into the current generation at a competative price.
The Xbox Series S features:
  • A cut down version of the same processor in the Xbox Series X
  • No disk-drive
  • An internal SSD with 500GB capacity
Both Xbox Series consoles play all the same games and run the same software.
Major Xbox Series Games:
  • Starfield
  • Goldeneye 007
  • Halo: Infinite
Microsoft has spent the last few years aquiring prolific game development studios. With a loaded portfolio of talented studios behind Xbox Game Studios, the Xbox Series consoles are sure to get a wealth of high quality first party titles in the near future. Many exciting titles have already been announced to be in development including:
  • Avowed
  • Fable 4
  • Clockwork Revolution
  • Gears 6
The Xbox Series consoles are struggling behind the bohemoth of Sony's Playstation 5, but are still a good option for gamers. Xbox's strategy recently has been to provide a great value to gamers and many options for how to play games.

The Nintendo Switch is the latest console released by Nintendo on March 3, 2017. Unlike Sony and Microsoft, Nintedo focused on making thier console's gaming experience unique by designing a "hybrid-console."
Nintendo Switch Features:

  • Play on the TV while plugged into the dock
  • Take the Switch out of the dock and play anywhere
  • Nintedo Switch Online Virtual Console
Nintendo has built a reputation for taking their time and releasing high quality, family-friendly video games that everyone can enjoy.
Major Switch Games:
  • The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild
  • Mario-Kart 8 Deluxe
  • Animal Crossing: New Horizons
Nintendo has dominated sales charts since the release of the Switch console. The Nintendo Switch offers a variety of experiences not available anywhere else, giving it a competative edge over the competition. By continuing to release polished, content rich titles, Nintendo has garnered a positive reputation in the games industry.

The Steam Deck was released by Valve on February 25, 2022.
Steam Deck Features:

  • 800p screen resolution with 16:10 aspect ratio
  • Access to a large library of PC games on Steam
  • Streamlined experience for players new to PC gaming
  • User repairability with parts available on iFixit
  • Ability to install and run custom software and operating systems made by third-parties
  • Customizability to allow for myriad user experiences
  • Multiple control options
Inspired by the success of the Nintedo Switch and other similar handhelds, Valve released the Steam Deck to a massive positive reception. The Steam Deck is a mobile gaming PC in the formfactor of a handheld console. Running SteamOS, Valve's custom version of the Linux operating system, allows the Steam Deck to achieve a "console-like" experience to the PC platform. This ease of use is opening the PC market to a new audience who were unwilling or unable to learn the complexities of running games on PC. The Steam Deck also provides several control options to allow users to play PC games that aren't compatible with controllers.
Control Features:
  • Gyroscope
  • 4 Programmable Rear buttons on the back of the device
  • 2 Trackpads on the front of the device
The Steam Deck has access to the whole Steam library of PC games. Unfortuntely, not every game runs on the Steam Deck for a variety of reasons. Fortunately, Valve has made determining if a game will work easy with Steam Deck Verification. Every game is assigned a rating on Steam to show the level of playability. Also, Valve's generous 2 hour return window ensures no one buys a game they can't play.
Top Steam Deck Titles:
  • Elden Ring
  • The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt
  • Hogwarts Legacy
  • Vampire Survivors
  • Red Dead Redemption 2
The Steam Deck is the most exciting new console on the market today.

The Playstation 4 was released by Sony on November 15, 2013. The Playstation 4 started with a strong impression on gamers by focusing on a "gamers first" marketing approach. This initial lead allowed the Playstation 4 to dominate sales charts for several years, until the Nintendo Switch was released in 2017. Racking up impressive console sales, the success of the Playstation 4 continued into its software lineup.
Best selling games include:

  • Marvel's Spider-Man
  • God of War (2018)
  • Uncharted 4: A Thief's End
While considered "old" by today's standards, Sony and many third-party game publishers still support the aging console today. Although major titles such as Spider-Man 2 will be exclusive to the newer Playstation 5, the Playstation 4 still has a little bit of life left in it.

The Xbox One was released by Microsoft on November 22, 2013. Following a controversial unveiling earlier in the year, the Xbox One stuggled to attract as wide of an audience as its direct competition, the Playstation 4. The controversy was about percieved anti-consumer practices commited by Microsoft in designing the Xbox One.
These practices included:

  • Requiring an online connection to play your games
  • Bundling the console with an expensive proprietary technology called the "kinect"
  • Blocking the selling of used physical game disks
After the dissapointing lauch, Microsoft appointed Phil Spencer as Head of Xbox in 2014 to "turn the ship around" so to speak. Under his leadership, many positive pro-consumer decisions have come from Microsoft.
Popular decisions include:
  • Backwards compatibility on Xbox One
  • Xbox Game Pass
